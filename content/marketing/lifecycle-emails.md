# Lifecycle emails (copywriting brief, tier 4)

Automated lifecycle emails. Replace [bracketed] placeholders with real values
at send time. No em dashes, no banned words, no exclamation points, no streak
guilt. Warm but plain. Every corporate email has a one-click unsubscribe.

---

## Welcome email (after a free individual signup)

**Subject:** you're in. here's where to start.

Hi [Name],

You signed up for promptdojo. Nothing to pay, nothing to schedule. The whole
course is open right now.

Here's the honest pitch for your first 20 minutes: don't try to learn python.
Just do chapter 00. It explains what an llm actually is and gets you running
one line of code. That's it. If that 20 minutes feels worth it, keep going.

Two things worth knowing:
- Your progress saves in your browser. No login needed unless you want to sync
  across devices.
- Not sure where to go after chapter 00? Pick a career path. Each one is a
  route through the chapters that fit your kind of work.

[start chapter 00]

[Josh]

## Inactivity reminder (corporate, after 14 idle days)

**Subject:** your [path name] path is right where you left it

Hi [Name],

You started the [path name] path on promptdojo a couple of weeks ago and
haven't been back. No problem, and no guilt. This is a reminder, not a streak.

You're [N]% through, on [last lesson title]. It saved exactly where you stopped.
Picking it back up is one click, and the next lesson is about [next lesson
topic], roughly [N] minutes.

[pick up where you left off]

If now isn't the time, that's fine. [unsubscribe from these reminders] and the
path will still be here whenever you come back.

[Josh]
