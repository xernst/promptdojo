# Email templates (Task 8)

Outreach and lifecycle email templates for promptdojo corporate. Replace
[bracketed] placeholders before sending. Conversational, short, no em dashes, no
consultant-speak. Subject lines included.

## Pilot proposal template

**Subject:** promptdojo pilot outline for [Company]

Hi [Name],

Here's the pilot, on one page.

**Who:** [team], about [N] people.
**Path:** the [path name] path. Exit outcome: [what they can do when done].
**Window:** [start date] to [end date], about [N] weeks. Learners go at their own
pace inside that window.
**What you get:** assigned paths by role, a progress dashboard, and a completion
report at the end.
**What success looks like:** we agree on this up front. A reasonable bar is
[X percent] of the team completing the path, plus a short feedback round.
**Cost:** [pilot pricing, scoped to the group]. No long contract to run the
pilot.

If [X percent] finish and the feedback is good, we talk about rolling it out
wider. If it doesn't land, you've spent one short window and a small budget to
find that out cleanly.

Want me to set the pilot up for a [start date] start?

[Josh]
